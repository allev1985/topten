Logo — usage rules
DoUse the wordmark on clean, uncluttered backgrounds. Maintain clear space of at least the height of the "m" on all sides. Use the icon mark at small sizes (< 24px).
Don'tStretch, skew, or rotate the wordmark. Change the two-colour split. Place on busy photographic backgrounds without a backing. Use the full wordmark at sizes below 80px wide.
DoUse the three approved colour combinations: primary on light, reversed on dark, on-brand on violet. The sparkle mark (✦) is optional but should appear in violet or lavender only.
Don'tUse the wordmark in any colour other than the three approved combinations. Add outlines, drop shadows, or glow effects. Place "MyFaves" or "MYFAVES" — it's always myfaves.
